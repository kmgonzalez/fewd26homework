October 1, 2016 by Karla

"...Should You Allow Comments on Your Blog?"

As I was building this page, I wanted to add a comment secton following my written pieces. I spent quite sometime learning how to create a comment box, which wasn't that difficult. The difficult part about it was learning out to retreive the comments which involves other programs such as PHP and others - out of the scope for the FEWD class. Moreover, while browsing through the internet I ran across the article, which I've attached the link above, debating if bloggers should allow comments on their site. Although, I agree with both sides of the debaters, I think I wanted to move away from having to monitor comments on my blog, not to mention the work I would have to put into on the backend of it.

Credit to Corbett Barr, author of the "Debate! Should you allow commens on your blog?..." article.
